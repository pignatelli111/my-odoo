Phase 0 — index & when to push
===============================

Push habit (updated): do **not** push after every small step sign-off in Odoo.
**Push when you move to the next phase** (or when a doc/runbook batch is ready
for the team): e.g. finishing Step 0.3 checklist in the repo and advancing to 0.4.

Runbooks:
  docs/PHASE0_STEP0_1_environment_roles.txt      — Step 0.1
  docs/PHASE0_STEP0_2_accounting_fiscal.txt      — Step 0.2
  docs/PHASE0_STEP0_3_core_apps.txt              — Step 0.3
  (0.4 Data migration plan — add when started)

Optional: if you change only sign-off lines in a runbook without advancing phase,
you may commit locally or batch with the next push.
