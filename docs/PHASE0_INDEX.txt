Phase 0 — index & when to push
===============================

Push habit (updated): do **not** push after every small step sign-off in Odoo.
**Push when you move to the next phase** (or when a doc/runbook batch is ready
for the team): e.g. completing Phase 0 (0.4) and starting Phase 1.
