Phase 1 — Step 1.3: Supplier master (runbook)
============================================

Goal: Every **active supplier** is a clean `res.partner` record with **supplier
rank**, **payment terms**, **lead time** data, and (light) **reliability /
comparison** fields so Phase 3 RFQ comparison does not start from empty data.

Prerequisite: Steps 1.1–1.2 (categories, UoM policy).

Git: **no `git push` for this step alone** — batch push when leaving Phase 1 or
at your milestone (docs/PHASE1_INDEX.txt).

A) Supplier vs contact hierarchy
--------------------------------
[ ] **One commercial supplier** = one `res.partner` with `company_type=company`
    (recommended) or clear parent/child if you use branches.
[ ] **Delivery / order contacts** as child contacts; purchasing defaults to the
    company partner for POs.

B) Supplier rank & flags
-------------------------
[ ] **Supplier rank** > 0 for anyone you can buy from; **Customer rank** only if
    they are also a client.
[ ] Deactivate (`active=False`) legacy duplicates after merging references.

C) Payment terms
----------------
[ ] On each supplier: **Vendor payment terms** (`property_supplier_payment_term_id`
    or equivalent on partner / accounting tab depending on version).
[ ] Align with real contracts (e.g. 30 end of month, 60/90). Same terms in Qonto
    narrative later (Phase 6).

D) Lead times (for RFQ / PO promises)
-------------------------------------
Typical Odoo sources (use what your version exposes):

[ ] **Delivery lead time** on supplier (days) — default expectation before line-specific overrides.
[ ] **Vendor pricelist** (`product.supplierinfo`): **Delivery time** per product /
    variant where it differs from default (e.g. glass vs profiles).

E) Reliability & comparison prep (lightweight in Phase 1)
---------------------------------------------------------
Until a custom model exists, pick **one** lightweight approach:

  (1) **Tags** on partner: `Affidabile`, `Sotto osservazione`, `Nuovo fornitore`.
  (2) **Priority** integer or `comment` / internal notes with a short scorecard.
  (3) **Custom fields** on `res.partner` (module) later: quality %, on-time %.

[ ] Optional numeric **Reliability score 1–5** in internal notes table (spreadsheet)
    until coded — if so, who updates monthly: ___________________________________

F) Fiscal & logistics on supplier
----------------------------------
[ ] **Fiscal position** or VAT if foreign suppliers (align with Step 0.2).
[ ] **Incoterms / default purchase incoterm** if you use them on POs.

G) Bank & legal (minimum for paying)
-------------------------------------
[ ] Bank account on supplier if you pay by wire (reduces friction later).
[ ] P.IVA / Codice fiscale for Italian suppliers where required for bills.

H) Smoke test (staging)
-----------------------
[ ] Create **draft PO** to 3 suppliers; confirm payment term and lead time appear.
[ ] Confirm **vendor bill** can be created with correct payable account.
