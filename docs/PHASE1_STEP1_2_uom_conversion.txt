Phase 1 — Step 1.2: UoM & conversion policy (runbook)
====================================================

Goal: One **canonical** policy for **mm / m / m² / pcs** (and any extra units you
use), with **pack / MOQ** defined at product or supplier level, and **defaults
per category** aligned with Step 1.1.

Prerequisite: Step 1.1 category tree agreed (or in progress for v1).

Git: **no remote push for this step alone** — batch push when you leave Phase 1
or hit an agreed milestone (see docs/PHASE1_INDEX.txt).

A) Canonical units (pick one primary per dimension)
---------------------------------------------------
Recommended **primary** units in Odoo for SBU-style work:

| Dimension   | Primary UoM | Typical use              |
|-------------|---------------|--------------------------|
| Length      | **m**         | Profiles, linear gasket  |
| Small length| **mm**        | B, H on estimate (field) |
| Area        | **m²**        | Panels, glass area       |
| Count       | **Units (pz)**| Handles, kits as “each” |

Rules to write in stone (edit):

[ ] **B/H on estimate** stay in **mm** in SBU module; **mq** computed in **m²** — OK.
[ ] **Purchasing profiles**: store and buy in **m** or **mm**? Decision: ________
[ ] **Glass / panels**: buy in **m²** or **m** of height? Decision: ______________

Avoid maintaining **both** m and mm for the **same** stock product without a clear
conversion — pick the **stock UoM** and convert purchase quotes in spreadsheets
only until Odoo packaging is filled.

B) Odoo UoM setup (Inventory → Configuration → Units of Measure)
-----------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] Ensure categories exist: **Length**, **Area**, **Unit** (Odoo defaults).
[ ] Under each, define only the units you need (e.g. mm, m, m², Unit).
[ ] **Relative factors** must be exact (e.g. 1 m = 1000 mm). Double-check rounding.
[ ] Odoo 19 note: if you use **relative UoM** links, follow the version’s model for
    “reference unit” vs “bigger/smaller” — one reference per category line.

C) Pack size & MOQ — where to store what
----------------------------------------
| Concept              | Where in Odoo (typical)              | Notes |
|----------------------|--------------------------------------|-------|
| Buy in packs of 6 m  | **Product packaging** (`product.packaging`) | Links to product UoM |
| Supplier MOQ / pack  | **Vendor pricelist** line / packaging| Per supplier |
| Min qty internal rule | Product **reordering rules** (later) | Phase 3+ |

[ ] For **top 20 purchased** items: set packaging or vendor MOQ so RFQ/PO lines
    default sensibly (even before automated demand).
