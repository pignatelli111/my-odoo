Phase 1 — index & when to push
===============================

**Do not `git push` when you finish an individual step (1.1, 1.2, 1.3, …).**
Push only when you **leave Phase 1** or at an **agreed milestone** (e.g. end of
Step 1.3 supplier master), so Odoo.sh does not rebuild on every doc tweak.

Local `git commit` is fine after each step if you want a clear history.

Runbooks:
  docs/PHASE1_STEP1_1_product_category_model.txt  — Step 1.1
  docs/PHASE1_STEP1_2_uom_conversion.txt         — Step 1.2
  docs/PHASE1_STEP1_3_supplier_master.txt         — Step 1.3

Phase 1 runbooks (1.1–1.3) are complete in this repo. **Push** when you want
Odoo.sh / remote to see them (e.g. before starting Phase 2).

Prerequisite: Phase 0 complete (0.1–0.4) for your environment.

Next: Phase 2 — docs/PHASE2_INDEX.txt (estimating / ANACO parity).
